Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 20, 2026

Your privacy is critically important to us. TapFlash is designed with a privacy-first approach. We understand that quick reaction time shouldn't require your personal details. This policy outlines exactly what we do and do not handle.

No Data Collection & Usage

The core philosophy of TapFlash is respect for user privacy. Therefore:

  • No Personal Data: TapFlash does not collect, store, track, or transmit any personal information (such as your name, contact details, or financial data).
  • No Analytics: We do not track how many levels you play, how many points you score, or any specific app usage patterns using third-party analytics services.
  • No Identifiers: We do not collect device serial numbers, advertising IDs, or any persistent unique identifiers.

Local Data Storage Only

The only data generated by your gameplay is your score progression and high score. This data is handled in a completely local manner:

  • Local Storage: Your game progression and high scores are stored entirely locally on your own device using standard iOS local storage (UserDefaults).
  • Data Ownership: This data never leaves your device. We have no access to your scores, and they are not transmitted to any server.
  • Deletion: You are the owner of this data. Uninstalling TapFlash will remove this locally stored high-score information from your device.

Full Offline Functionality

TapFlash is designed to work fully offline. The application requires no internet access, and we do not provide a way to register for accounts or login. No data is synchronized across devices.

Data Security

Since we do not collect, transmit, or process any of your data, there is no risk of a data breach on our part. Your local data is secured by your device’s own operating system and physical security.

Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. You are advised to review this policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this privacy policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

Acceptance of Terms

By using TapFlash, you accept the practices described in this privacy policy.
