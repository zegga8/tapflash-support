Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2026

Your privacy is critically important to us. TapFlash is designed with a privacy-first approach:

  • No Data Collection: TapFlash does not collect, store, track, or transmit any personal information, analytics, or user identifiers.
  • Local Storage Only: Your game progression and high scores are stored entirely locally on your own device using standard iOS local storage (UserDefaults). This data never leaves your device.
  • Offline Functionality: The game runs fully offline and requires no internet access or account registration.
